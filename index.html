Welcome to Sab Ahjar Studio
At Sab Ahjar Studio, we bring stories to life and craft unforgettable experiences through Game Development and Animation. Based in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia, we are a small but passionate team with a big vision: to redefine creativity and innovation in the MENA region. What We Do

Games that Engage and Inspire
We specialize in creating innovative, immersive, and memorable games that captivate players and keep them coming back for more. Every game we develop is a journey—a blend of engaging gameplay, stunning visuals, and unique storytelling.

Animation that Touches Hearts
Our animations are crafted to connect with audiences in meaningful ways. From concept to execution, we deliver creative, visually striking content that resonates deeply and leaves a lasting impression.
